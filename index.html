Stages of Cancer – Visual Walkthrough

1. Mutation Begins

A cancer cell begins multiplying rapidly. DNA is damaged, and the cell ignores the rules.

2. Immune Cells Approach

White blood cells like T-cells detect abnormal behavior and lock onto cancer cells.

3. Contact and Attack

Natural Killer (NK) cells make contact — ready to trigger cancer cell death.

4. Immune System in Action
